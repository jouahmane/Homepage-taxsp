GDPR Compliance

GDPR Overview

GDPR, or the General Data Protection Regulation, is a set of regulations aimed at protecting the personal data and privacy of individuals, especially those in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK.

How TaxSP Ensures GDPR Compliance

We take data privacy seriously at TaxSP and have implemented measures to comply with GDPR requirements:

  • We safeguard your personal data.
  • We provide transparency about data collection and usage.
  • We respect your rights regarding your data.
