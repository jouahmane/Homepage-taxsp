Q: What is the tax calculator?
A: The tax calculator is a tool that helps you estimate your income tax based on your income, deductions, and other financial factors.
Q: How do I use the tax calculator?
A: Simply enter your income and other relevant information into the calculator, and it will provide an estimate of your tax liability.
Q: Is the calculator accurate?
A: While the calculator provides a close estimate, it may not account for all tax factors. It's always best to consult with a tax professional for precise calculations.
